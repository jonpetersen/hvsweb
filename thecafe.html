The Café/Deli

The Café/Deli is open Monday to Sunday 08:30-14:00 with last orders for the café at 13:30

The deli-café menu is published on a blackboard outside the shop and includes hot pies and sausage rolls; sandwiches, wraps, baps and toasties; hot drinks, milkshakes, chilled soft drinks, iced coffee; cakes and tray bakes etc. Food and drinks purchased from the deli can be taken away or enjoyed inside the shop and at our outdoor picnic tables.
